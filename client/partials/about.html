about the Silex Index

The index helps you avoid the common problems of working with marketing services companies by helping you select the best marketing firm from the beginning.

A few of the common problems clients have working with branding and ad agencies, design and PR firms along with audio video production studios and media buyers are:

  • no knowledge of your company's industry
  • lack of innovative strategy and original creative
  • project delays and missed deadlines

Many times clients wish they could go back and select someone else, even though they probably picked the best marketing firm from the consideration set they compiled.

The Silex index solves this problem by drawing upon a database of over a thousand marketing service companies. Then we manage the selection process from consideration set to signed contract and onboarding.

Our purpose is simple: find the best marketing service firm for your company so you may achieve your growth goals. Choose the right marketing partner and your company will grow.

That's what Silex will help you do.
